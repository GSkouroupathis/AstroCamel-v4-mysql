FLY is CriticalSecurity.NET's LiveCD and first major project. The goal of the LiveCD is to offer members of CS.net both the chance to participate in a group project and expand their Linux and teamwork skills as well as to provide them with a lightweight Linux environment "rough and ready" as one member of the community has put it.

The current test release is version 0.5.4.
The download directory is available here.

To participate in the development of FLY, head over to the project's forum and the IRC channel.
