Quantum Computing - Quantum Logic

Welcome to our website, a portal to the basics of quantum logic. Through the following pages quantum systems and more specifically the logic behind them will be explained.

This project consists of three main sections:

  • A background in quantum mechanics.
  • The application of quantum theory in computing.
  • How classical logic has evolved in order to obey the laws of quantum mechanics.
